Depot Ship Repair Whole-ship availabilities (DSRAs, SRAs, CMAVs, DPIAs) executed at private yards under RMC-administered MAC-MO IDIQ task orders.
Hull, Mechanical & Electrical (HM&E) Ship mechanical and structural systems: propulsion accessories, pumps, valves, piping, HVAC, and diesel engines.
Combat Systems Sustainment Ship-borne weapons and fire control: VLS, guided missiles, launch and arresting gear. Includes Trident II sustainment carried by Ohio-class SSBNs.
Port & Technical Services Husbanding (fuel, transport, port visits), QC and inspection, OEM technical representation, and shipyard operations support.
Electronics & C4ISR Sustainment Afloat electronics: radar, sonar, communications, navigation, alarms, and instrumentation.
