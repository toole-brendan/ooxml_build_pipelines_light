Depot entry pipeline - MSRA to MAC-MO to task order
1
MSRA - Master Ship Repair Agreement
NAVSEA certifies that a yard's facilities, workforce, quality system, and safety credentials meet Navy depot-repair standards. MSRA is a prerequisite basic ordering agreement, not a contract to perform work.
Administered by: NAVSEA HQ via SUPSHIP offices Scale: ~25-30 CONUS yards plus ~10-15 FDNF foreign yards
↓
2
MAC-MO - Multi-Award Contract, Maintenance and Modernization
RMC awards a region-level multi-award IDIQ pool to MSRA-qualified yards. Awardees then compete individual availability task orders against each other.
Administered by: Regional Maintenance Center (SWRMC / MARMC / SERMC / NW RMC) Scale: 3-6 awardees per pool, 5-year PoP plus 5-year options
↓
3
FPTO - Fixed-Price Task Order
RMC contracting officer awards individual availability task orders against third-party planner specifications. Awardees bid against each other on labor hours and material pass-through for specific hull events (CNO avails, DSRAs, DPIAs, EDSRAs).
Administered by: RMC contracting officer Scale: per-availability, typically $20M-$200M per task order, 4-18 month duration
Note: (1) $4.9B in-scope TAM is $5.0B gross J998 and J999 obligations less $85M FMS carve-out; (2) Contractor segments: CONUS full-ship primes (BAE Ship Repair, GD NASSCO, HII / Metro Machine / Marine Hydraulics Intl, Vigor Marine, Continental Maritime, Detyens); Regional yards (East Coast R&F, Colonna's, Alabama Shipyard, Pacific Shipyards Intl, Bay Ship & Yacht, Bayonne Drydock, Tecnico); Technical services, non-shipyard (Epsilon Systems third-party planner, Amentum, Southcoast Welding, MAN Energy Solutions OEM support); Foreign MSRA yards (Sumitomo, Navantia, Hanwha Ocean, UniThai, Cabras Marine, Seatrium, Yokohama Engineering); (3) IDV scope taxonomy generated via LLM classification of 360 parent IDV descriptions and 471 residual no-IDV awards, applied to each J998 and J999 award; (4) Trade-specific IDIQs (HM&E, surface coatings, insulation, pipe lagging) account for less than 3% of depot spend.
Source: FPDS FY2025 contract obligations on PSCs J998 and J999 (U.S. Navy and U.S. Coast Guard), classifier-enriched. Data as of April 2026.
