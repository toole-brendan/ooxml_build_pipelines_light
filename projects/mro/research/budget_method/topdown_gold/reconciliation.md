## Section 2: External Benchmarks

### Benchmark 1: Total Ship Operations Context

- OMN Ship Operations total (1B1B+1B2B+1B4B+1B5B): $25,540,719K FY26
- Our TAM uses 1B4B ($13,803,188K) + 1B5B ($2,760,878K) = $16,564,066K from OMN
- Plus OPN ($6,571,211K) + SCN ($2,312,194K) + USCG ($847,966K) = additional $9,731,371K
- The OMN-only portion (58% of our TAM) is 65% of total Ship Operations -- reasonable, since 1B1B (operations/fuel) and 1B2B (training) are properly excluded.

### Benchmark 2: OPN Modernization Scale

- OPN BA1+BA2+BA4 total FY26: ~$14.2B
- Our Bucket 3 extraction: $6,571,211K (Navy only)
- Capture rate: ~46% of combined BA1+BA2+BA4
- The remainder is new construction equipment, shore systems, excluded items (Fixed Surveillance, SURTASS, training systems), and items not in scope. This capture rate is reasonable for an "in-service vessel work" filter.

### Benchmark 3: RCOH as % of SCN

- CVN RCOH FY26: $2,274,311K
- Total SCN: ~$50B+
- RCOH is approximately 4-5% of SCN
- This is consistent with historical RCOH share of the shipbuilding account.

### Benchmark 4: Public Yard Workload

- Four-shipyard OMN direct: $7,793,385K (from OMN Vol2)
- As % of Bucket 1 OMN: ~$7.8B / $13.2B = ~59%
- This is consistent with known submarine (nearly 100% public) and carrier (split) depot maintenance patterns. Surface combatants go to private yards.

## Section 4: Methodology Transparency

1. **Source:** FY2026 President's Budget justification books (Navy OMN, OMN Vol2, OPN BA1-4/BA5-8, SCN, USCG) plus NWCF for performer context.
2. **Taxonomy:** Seven work-type buckets derived from official Navy/CG definitions, budget structure, and contract language. See `docs/Seven_Bucket_TAM_Crosswalk.md`.
3. **Architecture:** Bronze (raw extraction from 9 books) to Silver (canonicalization, disposition, overlap resolution) to Gold (bucket classification, TAM table, reconciliation).
4. All figures in then-year dollars as presented in budget books (not inflation-adjusted).
5. In-service share estimates applied to PARTIAL items using program-level context from detail books.
6. USCG O&S vessel share (60%) is an analyst estimate pending CG-internal data.
7. Performer split uses OMN Vol2 shipyard data (PERFORMER_TAG), not additive to TAM.
