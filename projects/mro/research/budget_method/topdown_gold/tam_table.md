## Methodology Notes

1. **FY24/FY25 estimates for partial-share items:** Where the Silver data provided only an FY26 adjusted figure (e.g., "80% of total"), FY24 and FY25 values were estimated by applying the same percentage to the earlier-year totals, scaled proportionally from available data points.

2. **USCG allocations:** Coast Guard vessel maintenance spending is not broken out at the same granularity as Navy. Allocations between Bucket 1 (60%) and Bucket 2 (40%) for O&S 25.7 are analyst estimates based on USCG AC&I and O&S structure.

3. **Confidence levels:**
   - **High** — Directly traceable to a single budget line item with full FY24/FY25/FY26 data.
   - **Medium** — Derived from budget data with an analyst-applied in-service share percentage or an allocation split.
   - **Low** — Analyst estimate with limited source documentation.

4. **MSC Transfer:** The $1,584,000K MSC Maintenance & Repair line in Bucket 2 is new in FY26, representing a transfer from the Navy Working Capital Fund to direct OMN appropriation. This inflates the FY24-to-FY26 growth rate for Bucket 2; on a like-for-like basis, growth is more modest.

5. **SCN RCOH phasing:** Bucket 5 shows dramatic FY24-to-FY26 growth driven by CVN 75 RCOH ramp-up. This is normal life-cycle phasing, not a structural trend change.

6. **Bucket 1 (New Construction):** FY26 SCN includes $24.5B in OBBA/Working Families Tax Cut Act reconciliation funding. Many ship programs (DDG-51, LPD, LHA, MLS, T-AO) are funded almost entirely through reconciliation rather than the discretionary request. See `bucket1_newconstruction.md` for Request vs Reconciliation breakout by program.

7. **7-Bucket renumbering:** As of 2026-04-06, bucket numbering shifted from a 6-bucket (in-service only) to 7-bucket (full TAM) taxonomy. Bucket 1 = New Construction. Former Buckets 1-6 are now Buckets 2-7. The Silver Canonical file (`topdown/silver/canonical.md`) still uses the old 6-bucket numbering.
