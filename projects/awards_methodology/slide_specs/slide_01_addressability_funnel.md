# Slide Spec — Addressability Funnel

```text
SLIDE 01 - Addressability Funnel
Type: Body

Slide's purpose:
  Show how the entire federal awards universe narrows, gate by gate, to a short
  routed list of addressable opportunities — a MECE decision cascade whose dollars
  reconcile at the top. Folds in the two reframes: production money runs through OTAs
  (Saronic's own $457M lives at the consortium leaf), and almost nothing in the
  addressable set was ever advertised on SAM.gov Opportunities.

TITLE
-----
Copy:
  Addressability | The full awards universe narrows to a routed shortlist SAM.gov Opportunities never advertises.

HIGH-LEVEL LAYOUT
-----------------
A top-down MECE decision funnel (system map) filling the body. A central vertical
spine of stacked stage boxes descends from "all federal awards" at the top to the
route leaves at the bottom; each gate sheds a labeled drop-off bucket to the right
via a thin connector. Two waypoints are emphasized: the ADDRESSABLE MARKET band
(after the tier gate — the sizing denominator) and the ROUTE LEAVES row at the
bottom (the payoff). A reveal callout attaches to the consortium/OTA leaf, and a
single portal-visibility overlay strip spans beneath the route leaves. No chart, no
table — shapes and connectors only.

COMMENTARY COPY
---------------
Commentary 1
  Copy:
    An opportunity is not an open solicitation; awards data narrows the whole universe to a routed shortlist.
    The top gates are hard data (NAICS/PSC, dollars) and reconcile exactly; the lower gates (successor, route) are judgment, where the analyst adds value.
    The decision is route, not bid/no-bid: open bid, on-ramp, sub to a holder, or join the OT consortium.
    The production money and the recompetes both sit below the portal's waterline.
  Text / shape treatment:
    No-fill text rail on the right margin, bold finding sentence then non-bold
    bulleted support; sits clear of the funnel spine.

OTHER VISIBLE COPY
------------------
Copy (funnel spine, top to bottom — each stage box, then the gate that filters it,
then the drop-off bucket it sheds):

  ALL FEDERAL AWARDS
    (FPDS award types A-D and IDV, plus the SAM Contract Awards OT layer)
    gate: in maritime scope? (NAICS 33661x / PSC 19xx)
    drop -> Out of market (non-maritime)

  IN MARITIME SCOPE - 5,760 sea-service awards
    gate: addressable craft vs capital ships?
    drop -> Capital ships (broad maritime): $399.1B, 3,712 awards - excluded

  ADDRESSABLE MARKET - USV, small craft, other small: 2,048 awards, $11.5B
    gate: material (>= $5M)?
    drop -> Sub-scale (< $5M)

  MATERIAL BUYS (>= $5M)
    gate: future entry point? (ordering-period end <= 36 months, or recurring cadence)
    drop -> Locked mid-life -> Monitor;  Closed one-off -> Dead

  RECOMPETE CANDIDATES (ordering period ending or recently ended)
    gate: successor already awarded?
    drop -> Already recompeted -> Gone (re-enters at the successor's clock)

  OPEN TURNOVER (no successor seen)
    gate: reachable route?
    drop -> Incumbent-locked (no route)

  ROUTE LEAVES (all addressable, by route):
    Bid direct (open / standalone)  |  On-ramp (multiple-award)  |  Team / sub (holders-only, FAR 16.5)  |  Consortium / OTA

Reveal callout (attached to the Consortium / OTA leaf):
  Saronic's own $457M Navy OT (N000242596305, $296.5M obligated) lives here - invisible to the portal and to a standard FPDS pull.

Portal-visibility overlay (single strip beneath the route leaves, labeled as an
overlay, not a gate):
  Was any of this ever on SAM Opportunities? ~2% of notices touch USV; 99.9% of in-window addressable awards were dark.

Text / visual treatment:
  The spine boxes are neutral (no-fill or light gray, 1pt). The ADDRESSABLE MARKET
  band and the ROUTE LEAVES row are the focal family: a dark fill (blue, white text)
  with the one 1.5pt border on the slide, so the eye lands on the sizing waypoint and
  the payoff. Drop-off buckets are light gray, set to the right, visibly smaller. The
  reveal callout is a dark-blue connected callout; the overlay strip is a single
  full-width gray band, deliberately distinct from the gates. Bucket figures align so
  the reconciliation reads (5,760 = 3,712 + 2,048).

OBJECT NOTES
------------
  - Reads strictly top-to-bottom along the spine; the spine is the surviving set, the
    side branches are exits. Drop-off connectors are quarter to half point black with
    an arrowhead pointing OUT from the spine to each bucket; a short italic gate
    question rides each connector.
  - Keep the heavy/anchor (1.5pt) treatment to one family only - the ADDRESSABLE
    MARKET band plus the ROUTE LEAVES row read as one anchor family; everything else
    stays 1pt or no-fill.
  - The reveal callout connects to the consortium/OTA leaf (not free-floating) so it
    reads as "this leaf is where the money is."
  - The portal-visibility overlay is set apart beneath the leaves - it is a property
    of the addressable set, not a filter step; do not wire it into the spine flow.
  - Quantify only the top of the funnel - the market-sizing reconciliation (universe
    -> capital ships excluded + addressable, with the $11.5B / 2,048 anchor). Align
    those figures so the subtraction is legible. The lower stages (material buys ->
    recompete candidates -> route leaves) stay conceptual: no per-stage counts and no
    named vehicles. Do not add a fifth route leaf or extra gates.
```
