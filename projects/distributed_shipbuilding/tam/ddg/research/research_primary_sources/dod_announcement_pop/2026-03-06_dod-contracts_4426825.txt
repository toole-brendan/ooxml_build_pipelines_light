## Destroyer paragraph 1

Global PCCI, Irvine, California, is awarded a $50,000,000 cost-plus-award-fee and cost-plus-fixed-fee modification to a previously awarded indefinite-delivery/indefinite-quantity contract (N00024-19-D-4323) for the emergency ship salvage material system. This modification increases the contract ordering ceiling from $366,800,000 to $416,800,000. Work will be performed in Irvine, California, and other locations worldwide, and is expected to be completed by November 2026. No funding will be obligated at the time of award; all funding will be made available at the task or delivery order level as contracting actions occur. Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity.
