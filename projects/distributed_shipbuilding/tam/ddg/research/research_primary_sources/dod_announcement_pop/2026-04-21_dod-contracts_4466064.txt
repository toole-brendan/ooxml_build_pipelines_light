## Destroyer paragraph 1

Lockheed Martin Rotary and Mission Systems, Manassas, Virginia, is awarded a $36,973,224 cost-plus-incentive-fee modification to previously awarded delivery order N00024-26-F-6241 under an indefinite-delivery/indefinite-quantity contract (N00024-26-D-6203) to exercise an option for engineering and technical support in support of the Integrated Submarine Imaging System. Work will be performed in Manassas, Virginia, and is expected to be completed by March 2027. Fiscal 2026 research, development, test and evaluation (Navy) funds in the amount of $3,500,000 will be obligated at the time of award and will not expire at the end of the current fiscal year. Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity.
