## Destroyer paragraph 1

(Awarded Feb. 10, 2023) Gibbs & Cox Inc., Arlington, Virginia, is awarded a $39,659,692 cost-plus-award-fee and cost-plus-fixed-fee modification to previously awarded contract N00024-22-C-2313 to exercise options for supporting surface combatant ship design and engineering efforts for the Navy's Future Surface Combatant Force, primarily in support of the DDG(X) program, as well as other emerging ship concepts, and to conduct feasibility studies as part of supporting the broader Navy fleet. Work will be performed in Washington, D.C. (60%); Arlington, Virginia (35%); and Philadelphia, Pennsylvania (5%), and is expected to be completed by February 2024. Fiscal 2023 research, development, test and evaluation (Navy) funds in the amount of $2,646,941 will be obligated at time of award, and will not expire at the end of the current fiscal year. The Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity (N00024-22-C-2313).
