## Destroyer paragraph 1

Oceaneering International Inc., Hanover, Maryland, was awarded a $31,196,460 cost-plus-fixed-fee modification to previously awarded contract N00024-20-C-4315 to exercise options for continued support of the Navy&#39;s domestic and international submarine rescue program, Submarine Rescue Operations Maintenance Contractor. Work will be performed in San Diego, California, and is expected to be completed by September 2024. Fiscal 2023 operation and maintenance (Navy) funds in the amount of $6,811,591 (79%); and fiscal 2023 other procurement (Navy) funds in the amount of $1,795,226 (21%), will be obligated at the time of award, and funds in the amount of $6,811,591 will expire at the end of the current fiscal year. Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity.
