## Destroyer paragraph 1

Lockheed Martin Corp., Syracuse, New York, is awarded a $19,000,000 cost-plus-incentive-fee modification to the previously awarded delivery order N00024-19-F-6201 under indefinite-delivery/indefinite-quantity contract N00024-19-D-6200 for design and qualification testing of submarine electronic warfare equipment. Work will be performed in Syracuse, New York, and is expected to be completed by February 2024. Fiscal 2018 shipbuilding and conversion (Navy) funds in the amount of $3,600,000 (48%); fiscal 2021 shipbuilding and conversion (Navy) funds in the amount of $2,000,000 (27%); fiscal 2022 national sea-based deterrence (Navy) funds in the amount of $1,500,000 (20%); and fiscal 2017 shipbuilding and conversion (Navy) funds in the amount of $400,000 (5%) will be obligated at time of award and will not expire at the end of the current fiscal year. The Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C. is the contracting activity.
