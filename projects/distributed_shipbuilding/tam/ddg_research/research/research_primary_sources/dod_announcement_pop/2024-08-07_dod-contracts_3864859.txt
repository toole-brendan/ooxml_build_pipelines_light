## Destroyer paragraph 1

General Dynamics Electric Boat Corp, Groton, Conn., was awarded a $55,537,547 cost-plus-incentive-fee modification to previously awarded contract N00024-20-C-4312 to exercise an option for efforts related to completion of the USS Hartford (SSN 768) engineered overhaul. Work will be performed in Groton, Connecticut, and is expected to be completed by October 2026. Fiscal 2024 operations and maintenance (Navy) funds in the amount of $55,537,547 will be obligated at the time of award and will expire at the end of the current fiscal year. Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity.
