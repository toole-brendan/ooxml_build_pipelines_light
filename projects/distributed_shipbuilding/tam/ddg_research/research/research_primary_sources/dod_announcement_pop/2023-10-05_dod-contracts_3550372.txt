## Destroyer paragraph 1

(Awarded Sept. 29, 2023) Fincantieri Marinette Marine, Marinette, Wisconsin, is being awarded an $8,636,672 firm-fixed-fee modification to previously awarded contract N00024-20-C-2300 for procurement of long-lead time material for the land based engineering site for the Constellation Class frigate. Work will be performed in Walpole, Massachusetts (87%); Marinette, Wisconsin (7%); and Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania (6%), and is expected to be completed by Oct. 1, 2025. Fiscal 2024 shipbuilding and conversion, Navy funding in the amount of $8,636,672 will be obligated at the time of award and will not expire at the end of the current fiscal year. Supervisor of Shipbuilding, Conversion, and Repair, Bath, Maine, is the contracting activity.
