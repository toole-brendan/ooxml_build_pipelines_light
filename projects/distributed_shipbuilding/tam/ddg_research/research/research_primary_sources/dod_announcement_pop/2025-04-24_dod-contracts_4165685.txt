## Destroyer paragraph 1

Huntington-Ingalls Industries - Ingalls Shipbuilding, Pascagoula, Mississippi, was awarded an $80,739,428 cost-plus-award-fee modification to previously awarded contract (N00024-19-C-4313) to exercise options for the accomplishment of the Planning Yard Services for the Littoral Combat Ship in-service ships. Work will be performed in Pascagoula, Mississippi (83%); McLean, Virginia (10%); San Diego, California (4%); and Jacksonville, Florida (3%), and is expected to be completed by April 2026. No funding was obligated at time of award. Funding for work requirements discussed above will be provided on an incremental basis as needed throughout the period of performance. The Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity.
