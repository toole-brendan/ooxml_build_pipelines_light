## Submarine paragraph 1

Bechtel Plant Machinery Inc., Monroeville, Pennsylvania, was awarded a $69,235,114 cost-plus-fixed-fee modification to previously awarded contract (N00024-24-C-2112) to exercise an option for Naval Nuclear Propulsion Components. Work will be performed in Monroeville, Pennsylvania (61%); and Schenectady, New York (39%), and is expected to be completed by September 2033. Fiscal 2026 other procurement (Navy) funds in the amount of $11,209,535 was obligated at time of award and funds will not expire at the end of the current fiscal year. Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity.
