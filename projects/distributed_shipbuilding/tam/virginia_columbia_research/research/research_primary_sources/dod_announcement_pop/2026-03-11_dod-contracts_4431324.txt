## Submarine paragraph 1

General Dynamics Bath Iron Works, Bath, Maine, is awarded a $19,516,047 cost-plus-award-fee and cost modification to previously awarded contract (N00024-24-C-2331) to exercise options for the accomplishment of Zumwalt-class Planning Yard requirements. Work will be performed in Bath, Maine (99%) and other locations (less than 1% each, and collectively totaling 1%), and is expected to be completed by March 2027. Fiscal 2025 other procurement (Navy) funds in the amount of $9,895,750 (84%); and fiscal 2026 operation and maintenance (Navy) funds in the amount of $1,875,210 (16%), will be obligated at the time of award and funds in the amount of $1,875,210 will expire at the end of the current fiscal year. Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity.
