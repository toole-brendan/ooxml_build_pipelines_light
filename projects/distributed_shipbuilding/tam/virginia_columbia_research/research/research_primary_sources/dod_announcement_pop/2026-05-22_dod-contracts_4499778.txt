## Submarine paragraph 1

BAE Systems Norfolk Ship Repair Inc., Norfolk, Virginia, is awarded a $17,450,962 modification to exercise options on a previously awarded contract (N00024-26-C-4405) for the USS Iwo Jima FY 26 Selected Restricted Availability. Work will be performed in Norfolk, Virginia, and is expected to be completed by May 2028. Fiscal 2026 Other Procurement, Navy funds in the amount of $17,450,962 will be obligated at the time of award and are not scheduled to expire in the current fiscal year. Mid Atlantic Regional Maintenance Center, Norfolk, Virginia, is the contracting activity.
