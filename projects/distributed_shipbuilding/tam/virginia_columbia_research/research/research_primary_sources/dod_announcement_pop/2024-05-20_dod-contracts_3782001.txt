## Submarine paragraph 1

Oceaneering International Inc., Hanover, Maryland, is awarded a $17,775,259 cost-plus-fixed-fee modification to a previously awarded contract (N00024-20-C-4315) for continued support of the Navy&#39;s domestic and international submarine rescue program, Submarine Rescue Operations Maintenance Contractor. Work will be performed in San Diego, California, and is expected to be completed by September 2024. Fiscal 2024 operations and maintenance (Navy) funds in the amount of $5,256,607 will be obligated at the time of award, and funds in the amount of $5,256,607 will expire at the end of the current fiscal year. Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity.
