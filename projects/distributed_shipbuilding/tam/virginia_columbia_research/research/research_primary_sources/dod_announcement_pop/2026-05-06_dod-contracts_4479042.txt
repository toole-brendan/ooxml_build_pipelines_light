## Submarine paragraph 1

Nikira Labs Inc.,* Mountain View, California, is awarded an $11,091,577 cost-plus-fixed-fee, firm-fixed-price, and cost only modification to a previously awarded contract (N00024-25-C-4303) to exercise options for continued development, production, and installation for the Spectroscopic Total Air Monitor. Work will be performed in Mountain View, California, and is expected to be completed by April 2030. Fiscal 2023 shipbuilding and conversion (Navy) funds in the amount of $1,713,000 will be obligated at the time of award and will not expire at the end of the current fiscal year. Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity.
