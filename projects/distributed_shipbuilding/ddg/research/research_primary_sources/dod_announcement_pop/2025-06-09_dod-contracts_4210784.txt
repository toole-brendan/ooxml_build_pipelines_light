## Destroyer paragraph 1

Global PCCI, Irvine, California, is awarded a $12,200,000 cost-plus-award-fee and cost-plus-fixed-fee modification to a previously awarded indefinite-delivery/indefinite-quantity contract (N00024-19-D-4323) for the emergency ship salvage material system. This modification increases the contract ordering ceiling from $318,000,000 to $330,200,000. Work will be performed in Irvine, California, and other locations worldwide according to future delivery orders, and is expected to be completed by July 2025. No funding will be obligated at the time of award; all funding will be made available at the task or delivery order level as contracting actions occur. The Naval Sea Systems Command, Washington, D.C., is the contracting activity.
